Given the story or story summary identify a main character, the moral of the story, and the central topic of the story. These are defined as follows:

  • Main character: a central character of the story.
  • Moral: a short statement about the central lesson that is to be learned from the story.
    • E.g. Be kind to strangers; Slow and stready wins the race; Be careful what you wish for; Listen to your parents; etc.
    • Note that you do NOT have to agree with the lesson/moral, or support it! The moral is the lesson that you think the AUTHOR intended to convey.
  • Central Topic: main topic or theme that the story is concerned with. This is not the story genre.
    • E.g. Prejudice; climate change; family; etc.
Identify the BEST and WORST options for each category (except for main character). In some cases the options are very similar and the choices may be quite subjective. Use your best judgment to choose the single BEST and WORST options for each story or story summary.

Only treat options as the same if they are truly IDENTICAL (i.e. have EXACTLY the same wording and spelling, and are indistiguishable). Considerations for minor differences in spelling, grammar, or conciseness could be taken into account when selecting your best and least applicable options. We leave this up to you to decide. When there are identical options, please still choose different options for the best and least applicable questions (unless all options are identical).

Note that some of these tasks may be long because of the length of the text. All the same, please read the texts to understand them well, and reply thoughfully and with reflection.

Also bear in mind that some of the questions are "trap" questions, and are meant to indicate to us that you have adequately read and understood the stories. While many of the questions on this survey are highly subjective and can have many possible answers (which is actually the point of the survey), the trap questions are designed to be easy. They are such that if you have adequately read and understood each story, the answers will be straighforward.
